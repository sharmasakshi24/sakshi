Program Overview

This BCA program is designed to provide comprehensive education in computer applications. It is offered in partnership with NavGurukul, Eternal University, and SBA.NavGurukul offers a BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) program aimed at providing high-quality education in computer science to underprivileged students. The program focuses on practical skills and industry readiness, with a curriculum that includes programming, data structures, algorithms, web development, and more. It also emphasizes soft skills and personal development to prepare students for the job market. Key features of the NavGurukul BCA program include: Curriculum: Focuses on core computer science concepts, practical coding skills, and industry-relevant technologies. Industry Exposure: Opportunities for internships and projects with tech companies. Mentorship: Guidance from experienced professionals in the tech industry. Personal Development: Training in communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. Inclusive Education: Targets underprivileged students to provide them with opportunities for upward social mobility. NavGurukul also provides residential facilities, ensuring a conducive learning environment for students. The program is designed to be affordable, often with scholarships and financial assistance available for those in need.
